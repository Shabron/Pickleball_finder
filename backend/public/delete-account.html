Account Deletion Overview

We respect your privacy and provide a straightforward way for you to delete your Pickleball Finder account and all associated user data at any time. Depending on whether you have the app installed or would like to submit a request remotely, you can choose one of the options below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Instant & Recommended)

If you have the mobile application installed on your device, you can delete your account instantly by following these steps:

  1. Open the Pickleball Finder app on your device.
  2. Ensure you are logged in, then tap the Profile tab in the navigation menu.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of your Profile page and select the Delete Account menu option.
  4. Read the warning notice and confirm the deletion. Your account, profile details, and communications will be deleted instantly.
Method 2: Remote Request via Email

If you have uninstalled the app or are unable to log in, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to our support address: shauryamspp@gmail.com.
  2. Subject the email: "Account Deletion Request - Pickleball Finder".
  3. Include the name and registered email address associated with the account you wish to delete.
  4. Our developer team will verify and delete your account and associated data within 48 hours of receiving the request. We will send you a confirmation email once completed.

Data Handling Policy (Deleted vs. Retained)

When you delete your account, we perform a clean purge of all associated records. No data is shared or sold to third parties.

Data Category Deletion Action Retention Period
Account Credentials (Name, email, password) Permanently Deleted Immediate (No backup copies retained)
Player Profile Details (Skill levels, bio, play style, state, city) Permanently Deleted Immediate
Community Posts & Replies (Feed matches, discussion replies) Permanently Deleted Immediate
Direct Messages (Chat histories, conversation logs) Permanently Deleted Immediate
Notification Logs (Unread notifications, settings) Permanently Deleted Immediate
